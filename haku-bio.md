# Haku

![w](assets/Image_fx(160).jpeg)
"Clubs, finances, grades. So much to do, but I'll organize it all."

Haku is a white haired girl who is a student at The Academy.

She is a high-achiever, by-the-books and  Vice President and Accountant of her class.

She gets along pretty well with Ao and Midori, but thinks she needs to keep Aka and Kuro in check since they love flaunting the rules.

She has an affinity for holy magic.

If she went to Hogwarts she would be prefect no matter the House she got sorted into.

Loves:
- Keeping things organized

Hates:
- Disorganization
- Clutter

Relationships:

She thinks Aka is too impulsive.

She thinks Kuro is too power-hungry.
